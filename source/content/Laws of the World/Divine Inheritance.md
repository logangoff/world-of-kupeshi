## The Dimming
- The Dimming is the principal that over time, divine blood will wane, and magic will cease to exist from the world.
- [[Hanan]] was the first god. His offspring had 50% of his divinity. Their offspring 25%, so on and so forth. Hanan and his offspring have mostly disapeared from the world, meaning the purest form of god is only 25% (and they are trapped away). Because of this, magic is lesser than it was in the past, and if gods die or dissapear then over time, magic will vanish.
